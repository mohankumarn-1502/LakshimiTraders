Traditional Appalam Making

Established in 1989, we are a well-known manufacturer of traditional appalam and pappad, rooted deeply in the culinary heritage of the region. Our products are crafted using time-tested recipes and careful preparation methods that ensure authentic taste, crisp texture, and consistent quality.
